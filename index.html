About Us

Whether you just fancy a pub lunch or breakfast, have a meeting with business colleagues for coffee, are planning a special night or simply having drinks with friends and family, the White Hart, Little Waltham is the perfect choice.

Modern pub food and seasonal specials are all made from scratch using the finest, freshest ingredients & can be enjoyed alongside thirst quenching drinks, wines & cocktails.

Located in the beautiful village of Little Waltham, close to Chelmsford, the pub has been refurbished to provide a stylish yet relaxed environment suitable for everyone. The beautiful garden, heated Marquee & outside space can be used throughout the year for al fresco dining & drinking & customers are welcomed to use the online ordering system either indoors or outside; it couldn't be easier to enjoy a safe visit out.

The White Hart holds Weekly Offers, the best Sunday Roasts, Live Music, Children's Menu, Beer Festivals & more making it the ideal family local.

What's on?



Enjoy breakfast, lunch or dinner with a warm welcome, tempting cocktails, fine wines, well- kept ales & seasonal homemade menus here at the White Hart, Little Waltham.

Our reservations diary is open for bookings inside where you can enjoy full table service or in our heated marquee where food & drinks can be ordered from the bar via your phone – either way it couldn’t be easier to enjoy a safe visit out with friends & family



What’s On in June


11th June – 11th July Euro 2021

Follow every step of Europe’s biggest football competition live from the White Hart. Book your table from 11th June until 11th July & watch every game live in our heated marquee while enjoying your favourite White Hart drinks & snacks!



20th June – Father’s Day

Father’s Day is just around the corner & treating Dad to his favourite pub meal served alongside a perfect pint is guaranteed to put a smile on his face. Book your table now & bring the whole family along to enjoy some proper together time over great food and drink.

Loyalty Scheme


Pick up one of our loyalty cards and enjoy 5p for every £1 spent on Food or Drink either in house or take away on your Loyalty Card which can be redeemed against your bill at any time. Please pick yours up on your next visit.

Functions


Thinking of having a gathering? The White Hart caters for all kinds of events, from intimate occasions in our private dining room to larger celebrations in our Garden Room or Marquee. Our bright stylishly decorated pub is the perfect choice and we can guide you from initial enquiries through to the event itself.

Under current Covid restrictions we are able to accommodate up to thirty people in our Marquee on the same table. Inside we are able to accommodate up to thirty people for a wedding reception, wake or christening but seated on separate tables.
